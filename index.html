About Us

Clothes For Everyone is a student-led nonprofit in San Diego founded by Seung Soo Choi. We are dedicated to collecting, repairing, and redistributing secondhand clothing to underserved communities. We believe every piece of clothing deserves a second life—and every person deserves dignity and warmth.

Our Goals

We are committed to building a long-lasting system for clothing redistribution in the San Diego area. Our goals include:

  • Donating to multiple local clothing shelters including Sharia’s Closet, Father Joe’s Villages, and San Diego Rescue Mission
  • Creating a network of donors and volunteers to collect and sort clothing
  • Partnering with schools and community centers to host clothing drives and workshops
  • Raising awareness around sustainable fashion and the power of giving
  • Launching a mobile donation program to bring clothing directly to underserved communities
